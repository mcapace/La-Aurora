Connecting with Premium Lifestyle Enthusiasts

Partnership Overview

La Aurora partners with M. Shanken Communications to execute a targeted email marketing campaign through Cigar Aficionado and Whisky Advocate. This initiative connects La Aurora with passionate enthusiasts who appreciate premium tobacco products and fine spirits, leveraging the trusted relationships these publications have built with their subscriber base.
